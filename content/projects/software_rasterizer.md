### Introduction:
This was a project for CS 441 Intro to Computer Graphics at the University of Oregon and was written in C++. The project ties together topics
learned in class about how a GPU renders triangles to make more complicated models. Graphics techniques such as lerping, shading, and depth 
mapping are demonstrated.
